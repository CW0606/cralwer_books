<h1 id="chapter_title_177988"><strong>第一梦 红蝗 第05章</strong></h1>
<p class="info">
分类：<a class="cate" href="/novel-list-23.html" target="blank">文学小说</a><span class="divide">|</span>作者：<a class="name" href="/pdnovel.php?mod=search&amp;ac=author&amp;srchtxt=莫言&amp;searchsubmit=yes"  target="_blank">莫言</a><span class="divide">|</span>发布时间：2015-06-24 22:22:30<span class="divide">|</span></p>
